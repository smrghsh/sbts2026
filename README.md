# Save Salsa by the Sea

Community action site for [Salsa by the Sea](https://salsabythesea.com) — 24 years of free Sunday Latin dance on the Santa Cruz Wharf, now at risk.

The site makes it easy to email city council members and Parks & Recreation, with pre-filled email drafts and one-tap mailto links.

## Development

Static HTML/CSS/JS — no build step. Open `index.html` directly in a browser.
