Salsa by the Sea is at risk of losing its permit, and we need your help. We have received many complaints from surrounding businesses. Because of this, the city has removed us from the summer calendar, and we are at risk of losing more dates.

Our advisory board and permit holder are in communication with the city to negotiate and get us back on the calendar. As of right now, our last date is April 26. While our initial strategy was to work directly with them, we are now also discussing ways to bring more pressure by going to the city and wharf stakeholders about how important this is to so many people — because time is ticking.

We are working strategically, as our main goal is to get Sunday dates approved for the summer, as that has the greatest immediate impact on us as a community. Other goals and requests we have from the city are:

  1. Secure regularly occurring Sunday wharf reservations at an affordable rate at sunset for the remainder of the year.
  2. Support of our community as a protected cultural entity, potentially through a designated Latin dance day.

How you can support:

  1. Call and email representatives — this page makes it easy.
  2. Save the date: April 14 at 2:30 PM to attend the City Council meeting. A few people will be able to speak, but it's equally important to have a strong, visible presence showing calm and peaceful support. We know there are a lot of emotions around this. For many of us, especially Latinos and people of color, this feels inherently discriminatory. For the sake of strategy, we are asking that you show up as calmly and peacefully as possible.
  3. Please bring cash to donate to Salsa by the Sea, as fees have also increased significantly.
  4. If you have any skills that might be useful, DM us directly and include what you can offer.

This message was written by a few individual members of the Salsa by the Sea advisory board. It does not represent an official endorsement or position from the board as a whole. We simply want to ensure the community is informed and involved.
