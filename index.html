💃🌊 Save Salsa by the Sea 🌊💃

Salsa by the Sea has danced freely on the Santa Cruz Wharf every Sunday for 24 years — and now that's at risk. Email our city council and Parks & Rec to help us keep it going. Takes 2 minutes.

How to send

  1. Tap the red Send to City Council or Send to Parks & Recreation button — your email app opens with all addresses and the message pre-filled.
  2. Hit send — or add your name at the end if you'd like to personalize it

Button not working? Scroll to the bottom to copy and send manually.
